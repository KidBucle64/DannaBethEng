About us

Discover the heart of our story in every detail we create.
In this section, we present the origin and reason behind this amazing idea:

Dannabeth Handicrafts: "Handmade art!"

Transmitting love, sweetness, and charming through our crafts

In 2020, Dannabeth Crafts was established with a clear and special purpose: it was to create memories that endure over time, transmitting love and sweetness. During the pandemic time, our team took advantage of the opportunity to enhance our crafting skills and acquire new knowledge. This challenging period was a stage of growth and learning for us.

  • Creation of crafts that convey beauty and charm.
  • Continuous improvement of our skills and knowledge.
  • Appreciation from our customers, reflected in excellent reviews from them.

At Dannabeth Crafts, we take pride in knowing that our creations are appreciated for their beauty and charm among our customers, creating memories that last and convey deep emotions. We are committed to quality and excellence in every piece of art we create, and we look forward to share our passion for art with you.

Mission

Frequent questions

Here are the most common questions raised by our stakeholders and customers:

  • How long does it take to make an item?

    The time required depends on the complexity and detail you want to give it, but on average, an item can be crafted in less than 4 days.

  • Can I customize the design or color according to my preferences?

    Absolutely, all our products are customizable and adaptable to any taste and preferences.

  • What is the approximate price of a customized item?

    Clearly, customized items tend to be slightly more expensive than those in the catalog as they are tailored to the specific tastes of the client, usually with a small price difference of a few dollars from the common price.

  • How is shipping handled and how long will it take for an order to arrive?

    Products are delivered personally, or if you are from another city, shipments are made via Servientrega to anywhere in Ecuador.

  • What care should be taken to ensure the longevity of the item?

    The care required is minimal and specific, such as avoiding humid spaces and not applying any corrosive liquids when cleaning them.
